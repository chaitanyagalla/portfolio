## Decision Notes

Pick the version that best supports the actual goal: getting full-stack, AI-agent, or remote/hybrid opportunities.

This saved version is strongest when you want:

- A technical, serious, build-in-public feel.
- Proof through terminal replay, metrics, and receipts.
- A portfolio that feels more like an engineer's console than a personal brand landing page.

The other version may be better if it has:

- Stronger visual warmth.
- Better mobile polish.
- Clearer storytelling for non-technical visitors.
- More memorable first-screen composition.
